Enterprise Welfare Integration Platform

This is a SaaS platform targeted at small and medium-sized enterprises with 300-1000 employees. The core service is to integrate the benefits provided by the company, group insurance protection, and employee health profiles, etc., instead of employees gathering different information and welfare provided by the company from different systems or platforms.

We have developed three platforms for different uses:

  • Operation side: For systems administrators who are responsible for permission management
  • Business side: For platform managers (e.g., human resources) who manage the content of the platform
  • Customer side: For general employees of individual companies

Operation side

This platform is for systems administrators, who can:

  • Manage permissions (accounts) of platform managers
  • Control visibility of pages and menus on the business side and the customer side
  • Manage condition terms
  • Handle system notifications
  • Collect user feedback
  • Manage group insurance

The platform allows for the management of system-side operations, providing a GUI without directly modifying the database.




Business side:

This platform is for platform managers (e.g., human resources, welfare committee), who can:

  • Generate statistics of welfare (e.g., distribution, usage, insurance claiming)
  • Manage franchisees
  • Manage news broadcasts to employees
  • Approve welfare applications from employees
  • Generate labor health status statistics

The content on the customer side can be decided and managed through this side. The platform managers, who are usually from the human resources department or the welfare committee, can freely add or remove franchisee cards or welfare cards showing on the customer side. Additionally, if there are applications submitted by employees, the platform manager can approve or reject them here.




Customer side:

This platform is the main one and is supposed to be used by the most people compared to the other two platforms. This is for general employees who can:

  • Review the welfares, benefits, and subsidies provided by the company
  • Review the group insurance provided by the company
  • Review their health status
  • Find the franchisees that cooperate with the company

This platform provides the core services to users (employees). Here, they can review all the welfare and insurance information, etc., instead of gathering this kind of information from different systems and platforms. The main purpose is to solve the problem of information dispersion and the difficulty of knowing where to find the information.
